At Frey Water Rafting, we live for adventure. With expert guides and a love for the river, we create safe, fun, and unforgettable rafting experiences for all skill levels. Join us and make memories that flow for a lifetime

History

At Frey Water Rafting, our journey began with a single raft and a passion for adventure. What started as a weekend escape into the wild rapids quickly turned into a mission to share the thrill of whitewater rafting with others. Founded in 2020, we've spent years exploring rivers, mastering routes, and creating unforgettable experiences for families, friends, and thrill-seekers alike.

From humble beginnings to becoming one of the most trusted rafting outfitters in the region, we’ve stayed true to our love for the water, nature, and people. Every trip we offer is more than just a ride—it's a piece of our story. A story filled with laughter, adrenaline, teamwork, and memories that last a lifetime. Come ride with us and become part of the adventure.
